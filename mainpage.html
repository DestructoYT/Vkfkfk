Hi! Welcome to our newest game: Retros and the Tower of Doom. This is a single player game and the game is for you to pass the level.

Storyline

Bobby Slime, a covid effected cell he is stuck in the tower of doom. BOBBY is sick with covid and there is a vaccine in the tower of doom. Even though you cant cure viruses, Bobby still wants to help other cells in his village to prevent from getting covid. BUT, however, the tower was cursed by the evil spirits 10,000 years ago and Bobby must find the vaccine and get out of this tower before the timer ends. The clock is constantly ticking and you must help Bobby reach the end of the Tower before it is too late. Bobby needs your help!!!

This game is completely free so you dont need to pay for anything. However, you still need to have a computer, ipad or phone to play this game.
